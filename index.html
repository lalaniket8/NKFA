About

Curating elegant narratives across fashion, culture, and celebration.

Entrepreneur • Fashion Visionary • Creative Director • Cultural Advocate • Mentor Nandini Kumar is a name synonymous with elegance, vision, and creative distinction. With a rare ability to merge artistic sensibility with strategic insight, she has built an extraordinary career that reflects both substance and style. Her academic grounding in Science and Mathematics, formal training in Fashion Design in Mumbai, and entrepreneurial education from IIM Bangalore together form the foundation of a multifaceted journey defined by innovation, leadership, and enduring impact. With over 25 years of experience, Nandini has been instrumental in shaping and elevating fashion storytelling in India. As one of the early visionaries who introduced style and design into Indian homes through television, she played a defining role in transforming how fashion was understood, appreciated, and experienced by a wider audience. Her body of work spans the worlds of fashion, media, and entertainment, with deep expertise in conceptualising and executing projects across television, documentaries, films, and advertising. Her ardour for the arts and her commitment to the industry have driven her to collaborate with several renowned brands, including Rocky-Star, FedEx, Parle-G, Roopam, Shoppers Stop, LIC of India, Jashn Sarees, Kerala Tourism, and media houses such as Bag Films, Siddhant Cinevision, Cinevistaas Limited, A. K films, RAM GOPAL VARMA ENTERTAINMENT PRIVATE LIMITED, Maruti Films Pvt Ltd, DJ's a Creative Unit, and channels such as Sony Entertainment Television, Star Plus, Zee TV, Elle TV, Sahara television, various publications (Vogue, Women's Era, Cosmopolitan, Gr8 magazine, Society), and celebrities. Collaborated with renowned national and international brands, creating distinctive fashion concepts, styling solutions, and design-led experiences.
