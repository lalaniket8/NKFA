About Founder

Nandini Kumar

Director • Entrepreneur • Mentor • Fashion & Style Expert
25+ Years Experience Fashion & Styling Media & Entertainment Mentorship Entrepreneurship Cultural Advocacy
“Passion, willpower, and resilience continue to define Nandini Kumar’s journey.”

Nandini Kumar is a name synonymous with elegance, vision, and creative distinction. With a rare ability to merge artistic sensibility with strategic insight, she has built an extraordinary career that reflects both substance and style. Her academic grounding in Science and Mathematics, formal training in Fashion Design in Mumbai, and entrepreneurial education from IIM Bangalore together form the foundation of a multifaceted journey defined by innovation, leadership, and enduring impact.

With over 25 years of experience, Nandini has been instrumental in shaping and elevating fashion storytelling in India. As one of the early visionaries who introduced style and design into Indian homes through television, she played a defining role in transforming how fashion was understood, appreciated, and experienced by a wider audience. Her body of work spans the worlds of television, documentaries, films, and advertising with deep expertise in conceptualising and executing projects across fashion, media, and entertainment.

Her ardour for the arts and her commitment to the industry have driven her to collaborate with several renowned brands, including Rocky-Star, FedEx, Parle-G, Roopam, Shoppers Stop, LIC of India, Jashn Sarees, Kerala Tourism, and media houses such as Bag Films, Siddhant Cinevision, Cinevistaas Limited, A. K films, RAM GOPAL VARMA ENTERTAINMENT PRIVATE LIMITED, Maruti Films Pvt Ltd, DJ's a Creative Unit, and channels such as Sony Entertainment Television, Star Plus, Zee TV, Elle TV, Sahara television, various publications (Vogue, Women's Era, Cosmopolitan, Gr8 magazine, Society), and celebrities.

Collaborated with renowned national and international brands, creating distinctive fashion concepts, styling solutions, and design-led experiences.

Nandini Kumar's creative journey has extended beyond borders, showcasing her work on international platforms, including fashion events in London, Singapore, and the prestigious Indian Fashion & Fabric Fair in Germany.

Driven by a vision to inspire, mentor, and transform the creative landscape, Nandini Kumar founded NKFA (Nandini Kumar Fashion Agency) to bridge the gap between education and industry. Designed for the next generation of creators, NKFA nurtures talent, confidence, innovation, and professional excellence, empowering aspiring professionals to thrive in a dynamic global fashion ecosystem.

Inspired by India's rich artistic heritage, she also founded Artistiquetale—a platform dedicated to celebrating craftsmanship, contemporary art, and cultural storytelling. Through meaningful collaborations with artisans and emerging artists, Artistique Tale preserves and elevates India's creative legacy for modern audiences.

With over 25 years of experience spanning fashion, styling, creative direction, and luxury experiences, Nandini brings the same passion for excellence to her bespoke wedding curation services. Thoughtfully blending timeless elegance with contemporary sophistication, she creates deeply personal celebrations and curated trousseau experiences that reflect individuality, heritage, and refined luxury.

Vision & Mentorship

At the heart of Nandini Kumar’s journey lies an unwavering commitment to transformation — not only of style, but of people, perspectives, and possibilities. Her work is defined by a signature aesthetic that gracefully weaves together creativity, cultural richness, and contemporary relevance. Beyond her professional accomplishments, she has remained deeply invested in nurturing emerging talent and shaping the future of the fashion industry.

This commitment gave rise to NKFA, a platform envisioned to guide aspiring students beyond the boundaries of conventional education and prepare them for the dynamic realities of the fashion world. Through thoughtful mentorship, industry exposure, and practical insight, NKFA empowers young creatives to build careers that are confident, purposeful, and future-ready.

Artistic & Social Impact

Deeply inspired by India’s rich artisanal legacy, Nandini has consistently championed the preservation and celebration of traditional craftsmanship. Recognizing the importance of sustaining heritage in an ever-evolving world, she introduced Madhubani art on eco-conscious furnishings, bringing the intricacy and beauty of Indian craftsmanship to a global audience.

Her passion for art and cultural revival further led to the founding of Artistique Tale, a platform dedicated to promoting both traditional and contemporary art while supporting artisan communities. Extending this philosophy into the realm of social impact, she also launched The Craft of Mindfulness, an initiative that uses art and rural craft as meaningful tools for mental health awareness, healing, and community building among younger generations.

Philosophy & Legacy

Passion, willpower, and resilience continue to define Nandini Kumar’s path. She believes in the power of originality, in standing apart with conviction, and in leaving behind a meaningful legacy through every endeavour she undertakes. In a world that is constantly evolving, Nandini Kumar stands as a trailblazer across fashion, art, mentorship, and wedding curation — a woman of vision whose work continues to inspire those who aspire to create with purpose, lead with elegance, and dream without limits.

Artistiquetale

Designing beautiful spaces with conscious living. At Artistiquetale, we believe that luxury and sustainability can coexist beautifully. Our eco-friendly furnishing and décor curation focuses on creating spaces that are elegant, functional, and mindful of the environment.

We thoughtfully source sustainable fabrics, natural materials, handcrafted décor pieces, and timeless furnishings that bring warmth, character, and harmony into every space. Each element is carefully selected to ensure it reflects both aesthetic beauty and responsible design. Our approach blends modern design sensibilities with natural textures and traditional craftsmanship, creating interiors that feel calm, refined, and environmentally conscious. From soft furnishings and décor accents to curated styling details, NKFA transforms homes into spaces that are sophisticated, sustainable, and deeply personal.
