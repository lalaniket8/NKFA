Entrepreneur • Fashion Visionary • Creative Director • Cultural Advocate • Mentor
Nandini Kumar is a name synonymous with elegance, vision, and creative distinction. With a rare ability to merge artistic sensibility with strategic insight, she has built an extraordinary career that reflects both substance and style. Her academic grounding in Science and Mathematics, formal training in Fashion Design in Mumbai, and entrepreneurial education from IIM Bangalore together form the foundation of a multifaceted journey defined by innovation, leadership, and enduring impact. 
With over 25 years of experience, Nandini has been instrumental in shaping and elevating fashion storytelling in India. As one of the early visionaries who introduced style and design into Indian homes through television, she played a defining role in transforming how fashion was understood, appreciated, and experienced by a wider audience. Her body of work spans the worlds of fashion, media, and entertainment, with deep expertise in conceptualising and executing projects across television, documentaries, films, and advertising. 
Her ardour for the arts and her commitment to the industry have driven her to collaborate with several renowned brands, including Rocky-Star, FedEx, Parle-G, Roopam, Shoppers Stop, LIC of India, Jashn Sarees, Kerala Tourism, and media houses such as Bag Films, Siddhant Cinevision, Cinevistaas Limited, A. K films, RAM GOPAL VARMA ENTERTAINMENT PRIVATE LIMITED, Maruti Films Pvt Ltd, DJ's a Creative Unit, and channels such as Sony Entertainment Television, Star Plus, Zee TV, Elle TV, Sahara television, various publications (Vogue, Women's Era, Cosmopolitan, Gr8 magazine, Society), and celebrities. 
Collaborated with renowned national and international brands, creating distinctive fashion concepts, styling solutions, and design-led experiences. 
 
Nandini Kumar's creative journey has extended beyond borders, showcasing her work on international platforms, including fashion events in London, Singapore, and the prestigious Indian Fashion & Fabric Fair in Germany.

Driven by a vision to inspire, mentor, and transform the creative landscape, Nandini Kumar founded NKFA (Nandini Kumar Fashion Agency) to bridge the gap between education and industry. Designed for the next generation of creators, NKFA nurtures talent, confidence, innovation, and professional excellence, empowering aspiring professionals to thrive in a dynamic global fashion ecosystem.

Inspired by India's rich artistic heritage, she also founded Artistiquetale—a platform dedicated to celebrating craftsmanship, contemporary art, and cultural storytelling. Through meaningful collaborations with artisans and emerging artists, Artistique Tale preserves and elevates India's creative legacy for modern audiences.

With over 25 years of experience spanning fashion, styling, creative direction, and luxury experiences, Nandini brings the same passion for excellence to her bespoke wedding curation services. Thoughtfully blending timeless elegance with contemporary sophistication, she creates deeply personal celebrations and curated trousseau experiences that reflect individuality, heritage, and refined luxury.
Nandini Kumar is a globally recognised stylist and creative director whose work balances editorial precision with heartfelt cultural storytelling. Across fashion, media, and luxury experiences, her practice is rooted in elegance, intention, and enduring craft.
